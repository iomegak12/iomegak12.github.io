About Us

Redivac Farms: A Legacy of Excellence in Poultry Farming

Redivac Farms is a leading name in India’s, especially Tamil Nadu's poultry industry, specializing in the production and distribution of high-quality broiler chicken, eggs, and processed meat. With a strong presence in Bangalore and Madurai, we are committed to providing fresh, healthy, and hygienic poultry products to consumers while supporting rural communities through sustainable farming practices.

Our Vision

Empowering Rural India through Poultry Integration

At Redivac Farms, our vision is to empower rural India through our innovative "Poultry Integration" model. By fostering strong partnerships with local farmers, we enable sustainable growth and economic upliftment. Our mission is to produce nutritious, high-quality chicken and eggs while maintaining global standards in food safety and animal welfare.

Our Mission

Commitment to Quality and Hygiene

We adhere to the highest industry standards in poultry farming, ensuring that our products meet stringent quality and safety regulations. Our farms and processing units are equipped with modern technology to maintain optimal hygiene, providing consumers with farm-fresh chicken and eggs that are free from harmful additives.

Business Model

Win-Win Model, Empowering Rural Communities & Expanding Our Retail Network

Through our contract farming initiatives, we have empowered thousands of rural entrepreneurs, enabling them to build sustainable businesses. By offering financial and technical support, we ensure that farmers benefit from stable income opportunities while contributing to India's growing poultry industry.

To cater to the increasing demand for fresh and processed poultry, Redivac Farms has established a modern retail distribution network. Our strategically located outlets and supply chains ensure that consumers have access to premium poultry products, enhancing convenience and quality assurance.

Redivac Farms has pioneered the "Poultry Integration" approach, creating a mutually beneficial partnership between farmers and integrators. This model provides farmers with the necessary resources, technical support, and market access while ensuring a consistent supply of high-quality poultry for our customers. Our integration system has contributed significantly to rural employment and economic stability.

Our Practices

Sustainable and Ethical Farming Practices & Leadership and Expertise

Sustainability is at the heart of our operations. We follow responsible farming practices that prioritize animal welfare, environmental conservation, and efficient resource utilization. Our farms operate with a commitment to reducing carbon footprints while maintaining the highest production standards.

Our continuous investment in infrastructure, technology, and research ensures our sustained growth and ability to meet the evolving needs of the poultry industry.

Under the dynamic leadership of our Executive Director, Jooty Dhamodharan Ramkumar, and Director, TJ Chitra, Redivac Farms continues to set new benchmarks in the poultry sector. Their strategic vision and industry expertise drive our commitment to excellence and innovation.

Long Term Goals

The Future of Redivac Farms

As we look ahead, Redivac Farms is focused on expanding its market reach, enhancing production capabilities, and further strengthening rural development initiatives. With a steadfast commitment to quality, sustainability, and community empowerment, we aim to become a household name in India's poultry industry, ensuring healthier lives for generations to come.
